hi, welcome to my page!

after two years of clinical research at Mass General Brigham developing biosensor-based machine learning models for relapse prevention in substance use disorder, i've been inspired to return for a master's in computer science focused on efficient ML for biosignal processing, as well as wearable device design for sensitive clinical populations.

i am fortunate to be advised by Paul Pu Liang at the multisensory intelligence group within MIT Media Lab. my technical research is molded by strong ethical principles here and from my media studies background, with emphasis on biometric data privacy, accessible design, and mitigating unhealthy relationships in human-computer interaction.

in my free time, i like to build games and make art! constructive feedback is always welcome :)
